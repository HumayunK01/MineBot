OUR SERVICES

Guidance

he chatbot can provide information on various safety regulations and acts relevant to mining operations. It can guide miners on how to comply with these regulations and ensure workplace safety.

Reference Assistance

The chatbot can provide quick access to relevant safety regulations and acts applicable to mining operations. Miners can query the chatbot to obtain specific information on rules governing aspects such as ventilation, equipment safety standards, or hazardous materials handling.

Compliance Checklists

The chatbot can generate customizable checklists based on safety regulations, allowing miners to ensure that all necessary safety measures are in place before starting work.

Safety

Miners can subscribe to regular reminders and updates on safety procedures provided by the chatbot. These reminders can include daily safety checks, equipment maintenance schedules, and notifications about upcoming safety training sessions or regulatory deadlines.

Q&A Sessions

The chatbot can host interactive question-and-answer sessions where miners can seek clarification on specific safety regulations or seek advice on compliance-related issues. The chatbot can provide instant responses to queries and direct miners to additional resources or expert guidance when needed.
